Expert Plus is a mod which adds new accessories based on normal expert accessories improving on them.
Includes language support for English & French

Items Include[
	Confusing Probe
	  20% Chance to dodge an attack
	  dodging an attack summons illusion probes

	Destroyer Collar
	  +15% Damage Reduction
	  6 Armor

	Gooey Alliance
	  Slimes will not attack you
	  Shoots balls of gel and crystals

	Retinazer's Tower Shield
	  +6 Defenese
	  Allows the user to dash
	  Dashing into an enemy will shoot an explosive change with heavy recoil

	Spazmatism's Buckler
	  +4 Defense
	  Allows the user to dash
	  Dashing into an enemy inflics cursed inferno and broken armor
]
